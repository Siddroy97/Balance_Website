mission

mission

We exist so small clinics can treat patients instead of wrangling paperwork. When a neighborhood pizza joint can tap a card reader for instant payment, doctors shouldn't need a six-page "crust necessity form" just to get paid for a sore-throat visit. Balance Health turns every doctor-patient conversation into clean, payer-perfect data—so care (and cash) flows as fast as ordering that slice.

why it matters

  • $175 billion+ in annual admin waste keeps care under-funded.
  • Physicians juggle ≈39 prior-auth errands and 13 lost hours each week.
  • Denial rework costs $25–$117 per claim, yet up to 60 % never get fixed.

why small clinics feel it first

Independent practices run on razor-thin margins. Post-COVID staffing shortages and rising payer demands have made admin overhead their No. 1 threat. Every delayed approval means slower care and shaky cash flow.

what we're building

  1. listen – HIPAA-secure AI scribe captures the visit in real time.
  2. validate – Policy agents tag codes, check necessity, and launch prior-auth on the spot.
  3. submit – FHIR-native claims fly to clearinghouses before checkout.
  4. learn – Denial feedback trains the model, boosting first-pass acceptance.

early results

  • Auth turnaround: 1–3 days → minutes.
  • Tens of thousands in recovered revenue per clinic.
  • One full admin day a week returned to providers.
