Automated from conversation to claim

Balance Health is an in-clinic app that listens, transcribes doctor notes, uploads them to your EHR, and auto-generates insurance claims — so you get paid faster, with less admin.

What we're solving with Balance

Today, small clinics struggle with overwhelming administrative tasks. Manual transcription of notes, insurance pre-authorizations, claim denials, and delayed payouts leave staff burned out and patients underserved. Balance Health exists to automate the entire clinical-to-claims process — enabling clinicians to focus on care, not paperwork.
